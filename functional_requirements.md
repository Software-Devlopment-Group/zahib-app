## Functional Requirements

1. The system shall allow users to register and log in securely.
2. The system shall display the food menu with available items and prices.
3. Users shall be able to browse menu items before placing orders.
4. Users shall be able to place food orders through the system.
5. The system shall manage customer orders in a queue.
6. Users shall be able to track their order status in real time.
7. Users shall be able to view their order history.
8. Users shall be able to rate completed orders.
9. Staff shall be able to view incoming orders.
10. Staff shall be able to update order statuses.
11. Admin shall be able to manage menu items.
12. Admin shall be able to generate and view reports.
13. The system shall store all order and user data in the database.
